Inside each tasting spread, you will find guided fields for color and clarity, nose and aroma, body, tannin and acidity levels, finish, and a full personal rating scale. It is the kind of master wine tasting journal that transforms a good bottle into lasting knowledge.

Inspired by global tasting standards, this wine tasting journal guide walks you through the same systematic approach used by professionals — making it equally powerful for a newcomer learning the ropes and a seasoned enthusiast refining their palate. With 160 pages across a generous 8 x 10 format, there is plenty of room to record detail without cramming notes into tiny boxes.

The color and clarity comparison charts at the front set the stage for a deeper tasting experience. Detailed flavor mapping pages help you move from vague impressions ("I taste something fruity") to precise descriptions ("dried cherry, cedar, medium+ tannin").
