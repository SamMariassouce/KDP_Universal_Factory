The Wine Student's Tasting Journal is built specifically for anyone preparing for WSET Level 2, Level 3, or similar wine certification exams. Where most journals are designed for casual enjoyment, this wine tasting journal wset students will actually use in their studies — structured around the same systematic approach to tasting that certification courses teach.

Each of the 120 assessment sheets follows a rigorous, exam-ready format: appearance, nose, palate, and conclusions — with dedicated fields for all the descriptors you need to know cold before test day. This is the wine tasting journal guide that bridges the gap between classroom instruction and real tasting practice.

The 20 blind tasting practice scoresheets at the back are where the real preparation happens. Blind tasting is one of the most challenging parts of any wine certification exam, and consistent practice is the only way to build the confidence you need. Fill in a sheet before you check the label, then review your reasoning — this is exactly how professional wine certification study journal practice should work.

At 8.5 x 11 inches, the large format gives every assessment field the room it needs. No squinting at tiny boxes or cramming notes into margins.
