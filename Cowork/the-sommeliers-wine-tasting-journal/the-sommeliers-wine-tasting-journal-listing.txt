DESCRIPTION (copy into KDP Description field)
--------------------------------------------------------
Every great wine tells a story. Whether you are standing in a Burgundy cellar, attending a trade tasting, or visiting a winery on the road, capturing the moment with precision is what separates a professional from a casual drinker.

The Sommelier's Wine Tasting Journal is built for people who take their palate seriously. This is a detailed wine tasting journal designed for industry professionals, winery staff, certified sommeliers, and advanced enthusiasts who need structured, reliable pages — not a generic notebook.

Each entry guides you through a full evaluation: appearance, nose, palate, tannin structure, acidity, body, finish, and overall rating. This is the tasting wine journal format used in formal certification environments, now in a compact pocket-friendly size you can carry anywhere.

Inside you will find:
• Vineyard and Producer Information Logs to capture the source behind every bottle
• Technical Tasting Grids with calibrated scales for Tannin, Acidity, Body, and Finish
• Business Contact Directory for winery connections and industry networking
• 100 pages of structured tasting entries — enough for a full season of serious work

Whether you use it as a wine tasting and drinking journal at vineyard visits, a spirits tasting journal for cross-category tastings, or a wine journal for tasting events and trade shows, this sommelier log book adapts to your workflow. Its 5 x 8 pocket size slips into a jacket or kit bag without adding bulk.

This is not another decorative wine notebook. This is a professional tool for a professional palate.
